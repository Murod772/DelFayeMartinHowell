Delegate Faye Martin Howell is:

  • A life-long resident of District 24
  • A member of the Maryland General Assembly Judiciary Committee
  • A member of the Maryland Legislative Black Caucus
  • A member of Women Legislatures of Maryland
  • Former Treasurer and current member of the Prince George’s County Democratic Central Committee
  • Community Liaison for the Cora Rice – Prince George’s County Police – Community Party for Deserving Children
  • Assistant Treasurer of the Coalition of Central Prince George’s Community Organizations
  • A lifetime member of the University of Maryland Eastern Shore (UMES) Alumni Association (BS in Math & Computer Science)
  • A lifetime member of Fairmont Heights High School Alumni Association
  • A lifetime member of Frontiers International, Inc. (oldest black service organization in the US)
  • Former Treasurer for Friends of Joanne C. Benson
  • President of the Tri-Area Civic Association
  • Board member of the Glenarden Track Club
  • Member of the First Baptist Church of Glenarden
  • A dedicated community activist with over 50 years of community activism and volunteer service
  • The mother of two and the grandmother of 10

A Proven Record of Service and Leadership

24th District Representative


As your 24th District representative in Annapolis, Delegate Howell has been serving you and working to:

  • Create better paying jobs
  • Invest in our public schools and build a better future for our children
  • Increase affordable housing options
  • Expand access to affordable health care
  • Bring more grocery stores to District 24
  • Fight for tax breaks for our senior citizens
  • Make childcare more accessible and affordable
  • Make government accountable to you
